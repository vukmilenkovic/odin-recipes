Chicken Alfredo

Description

This is a wonderful chickn pasta recipe that will make you fall asleep faster than melatonin because of the heaviness of it's ingredients.

Ingedients

  • Chicken
  • Pasta
  • Parmegianno reganno
  • Eggs
  • Heacy cream
  • Salt
  • Peper

Steps

  1. First, we're going to cook our chicken breasts before preparing our pasta.
  2. Once done, transfer to a cutting board, let it rest for 10 minutes, then slice and set aside.
  3. Then add the broth, milk, and garlic to a pan, then season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bring to a simmer, then add the fettuccine.
  5. Cook until barely al dente, then stir in the Parmesan and cream.
  6. Once done, remove from the heat and add the chicken.
