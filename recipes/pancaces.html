Balkan Pancaces

Description

This is the ORIGINAL pancace recipe and it's a stample in every Balkan and most of the eastern european cuisine!

Not to be mistaken for American PAncaces!!! These are not even close...

Ingedients

  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Put 3 eggs in a large container
  2. Add a spoon of sugar
  3. Add the flour
  4. Mix until it has a thick creamy texture
  5. Add milk until the mass gets thiner
  6. Now repeat the previus two steps until you get a nice thin mass that you will pour int a hot pan and make palacinke.
