Bolognese

Description

This is a sphagetti bolognese recipe, a lovely combination of moist tomatoes and dry meet, you'll love it!

Ingedients

  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Ground beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Peper

Steps

  2. Put Onions in the pan and frie untill nice golden colour/
  3. Put the cut carrots in the pan with the onions
  4. Put the meat in the pan along with the other ingredients
  5. Season the ingredients with seasonings of choice, add a little water ant cover the pan. Put the stove on low-medium heat
